Top 5 Best Movies of All Time

Joko's Movie Picks

Knives Out

A spectacular murder mystery/family drama. A must watch for broken families everywhere!

Train to Boson

*sniff* It's a zombie movie that really goes after your heart. Bring some hankies
along with that bucket of popcorn.

Crazy Rich Asians

This movie is described as a cinderella movie. This movie provides so much more,
with it's breakdown of the divide between haves and have nots and what it means for
these worlds to intertwine from the perspective of an asian culture.

Cat Returns

One of Studio Ghibili's finest movies. It has talking cats and a high school girl
with a questionable love interest. Also, the visuals and music are some of the finest
the studio has to offer.

Slotherhouse

This slasher film has everything:

  • Bratty sorority girls
  • Slashing
  • A pointless shower scene
  • And... a killer sloth?
Yup you heard that right, a killer sloth. Murderers in horror films don't typically move
very fast to begin with, so why shouldn't the killer be a sloth. 10/10, would recommend!
